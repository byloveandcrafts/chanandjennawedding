Need to Know

Wedding FAQs

Everything you need to know to make the most of our special day together.

Unplugged Ceremony

We kindly ask that you silence your phones and keep all devices away during our ceremony. Our photographer is there to capture every sacred moment — so please be present with us, not behind a screen.

📷 Photography and videos are warmly welcomed during the reception! We'd love to see the celebration through your eyes. Don't forget to share your photos to our guest album.

We recommend arriving by 2:00 PM so you can settle in, find your seat, and enjoy the atmosphere before our 3:00 PM ceremony begins. The church doors will be open and our ushers will be ready to welcome you. Please avoid arriving right at 3:00 PM so you don't miss the processional entrance.
Yes! Our dress code is semi-formal attire in our motif palette: champagne, sand, blush pink, and gray. Ladies are encouraged to wear elegant gowns or semi-formal dresses in these tones. Gentlemen are encouraged to wear barong Tagalog or a formal suit. Please avoid wearing white, off-white, or black to the ceremony as a courtesy to the bride. Check our Attire Guide section for more inspiration.
Yes — we are having an unplugged ceremony. We ask all guests to keep their phones and cameras stored away during the ceremony itself so that everyone can be fully present in this sacred moment. Our professional photographer will capture everything beautifully. However, please feel free to take all the photos and videos you want during the cocktail hour, reception, and party!
Our celebration is an intimate affair with a limited guest list. Please refer to the seat count indicated on your invitation. If you received an invitation for one, we kindly ask that you attend solo. If you have questions about your seat count or need to discuss arrangements, please reach out directly to the couple before the RSVP deadline of June 13, 2026.
Once the seating chart is finalized, you can use the Seat Finder section on this website to view your assigned table. On the day of the wedding, our ushers at the church and reception venue will also be happy to guide you to your seat. Name cards will be placed at each table setting at the reception.
The reception will be held at Costa Abril Events Venue, located along Riverside Rd, Rodriguez, Rizal. The venue is approximately a 5–10 minute drive from the church. Cocktails and snacks begin at 4:30 PM, followed by dinner and the program at 6:00 PM. Check our Venues section for the map directions.
Parking is available at both the church and reception venue. We recommend carpooling where possible to make things easier for everyone. If you're coming from farther away, consider arranging a ride-share or designated driver so the whole group can enjoy the celebration worry-free.
Absolutely! We'd love to see the celebration through your eyes. During the reception, please feel free to snap and share all the beautiful moments. Use our wedding hashtag when posting so we can find and cherish your photos. You can also upload directly to our shared guest album — check the Photo Album section for details.
We completely understand that life happens. If you're unable to join us on our special day, please let us know as soon as possible by using the RSVP form and selecting "Unable to attend." We'd still love to receive your warm wishes through the digital guestbook on this site. Your love and blessings mean the world to us, whether near or far.
